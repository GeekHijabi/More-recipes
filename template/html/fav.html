Turkish Salad

Cut the red onion in half, peel and finely slice it. Then toss all the chopped veggies into a large bowl. To make the dressing crush, peel and finely mince the clove of garlic. Add to it the juice of a large lemon, salt, pepper, olive oil, the finely chopped mint and parsley and sumac.
